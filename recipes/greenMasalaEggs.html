Delicious eggs, rice and Masala

This is a simple egg and rice dish, high in protein(22g/serving)

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 350g Brown basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp rapeseed oil
  • 1 onion, finely sliced
  • 2cm piece ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 400ml can coconut milk
  • 2 green chillies
  • 1 tsp mango chutney
  • small pack coriander

Steps

  1. Cook eggs 6 mins in boiling water, then flash cool in cold water. Cook Rice according to pack instructions
  2. In frying pan, cook onions in oil until soft. Add ginger & cook 1 min. add turmeric, chilli powder, and coriander and cook 1-2 mins until fragrant. Add coconut milk, most of chillies & mango chutney. Bring to simmer & cook 5 mins.
  3. In separate pan, heat oil. Peel & add eggs until brown & starting to bubble and crisp. Carefully remove and halve
  4. Rough chop coriander and stir most into masala with some salt. Add eggs, scatter remaining chilli & corinander over top, and serve over rice.
