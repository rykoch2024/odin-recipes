Homemade Mozzarella

Home

Ingredients

  • Whole milk
  • Acid, preferably wite vinegar at 5%, if not, lemon juice at 5%
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring milk to 115 deg F
  3. turn off heat
  4. Add acid
  5. Stir 2-3 mins until curds separate from whey
  6. Note: if whey is still white, add 1 tbsp acid & stir until golden color
  7. Remove curds, cutting if needed due to size or too solid
  8. Squeeze to remove whey
  10. Add salt to whey, heat to 170
  11. Press cheese into ball & add to whey, 2-3 mins
  12. Alternate: heat in microwave 20 seconds until cheese reaches 170
  13. Strech (use spoon if too hot) until smooth. Do not overstretch
  14. Form and chill. Good 1-2 weeks in fridge when wrapped in cling or air-tight container
  15. Note: leftover whey can frozen for use in soups or smoothies
